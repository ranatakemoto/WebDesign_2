OUR MISSION

The Surf Bus Foundation's mission is to empower people to have a healing connection to the sea.
We do this by engaging in ocean sports like surfing, swimming and beach games while exploring the wonder and beauty of the sea. Our teaching and mentoring provides understanding that encourages a lifelong commitment to share and protect our oceans for all to enjoy.
